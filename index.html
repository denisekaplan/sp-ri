Everyone is Unique

Sherry

Reimagine gave me a sense of not being isolated and separated from the rest of the world. I felt more connected and encouraged by hearing other people who were also going through the same things I was going though.

Erica

There is a real power in knowing how to heal and in having the tools to feel btter... When I began using the tools and basic sills in my own life, I discovered a depth and magic held within them that create immediate relief, seemingly spontaneous hope, and a new understanding of myself.

Helen

I felt like cancer was just taking over my life and I wasn't Helen anymore, I was Helen with cancer. It took a little while, but I found my happy spot again. I found myself again. I stopped fearing that every ache in my body was going to be cancer coming back.

Reimagine is life changing. It changed the foundation of who I am. People see me, they think, "Oh, well, this is what cancer surgery did for you." No, that was the Pillars. Pillars said redefine yourself, reimagine and then that really is it."
